About Me

The Early Days: I was born in April of 2001 G.C, in a small town called Hawassa. I lived there all my life but moved to Addis Ababa, the capital of Ethiopia, in 2014. I attended and graduated high school from school tomorrow and now, I am currently enrolled and attending collage at Addis Ababa University (AAU). So, in addition to being a food enthusiast I am also an aspiring creative designer and web developer with a focus on frontend technologies.

Anyone who knows me, knows that I take school and education really seriously. And for everyone who asks me why I do not drop out of school and pursue cooking fool time my answer is;
I can be a full time software engineer and still cook, but I can not be a full time chef and be a software engineer too.

I am also extremely family oriented. My family is everything to me and I cherish my interactions and affections with them more than anything in this world. I have my parents and then my younger siblings; a sister and two brothers. With cousins somwhere in the uncountable ranges lol.

So, that’s me. I hope reading that was informative or at the very least made you feel amused as it did me when I was writing it.

A cookie is a small file which asks permission to be saved onto your device when you visit our Website. Once you agree, the file is added.
