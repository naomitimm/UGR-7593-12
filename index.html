About Naomi Timothy

Hello my name is Naomi Timothy. I am a self taught cook and food enthusiast, currently living in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. If I'm not slaving away at the library, since that is what software engineering students seem to do, I am slaving away at the kitchen.

The thing I love most about food is the interaction between people. Whether that is cooking with friends or family, teaching someone to cook, or that feeling when they try your food.
For me, cooking is almost therapeutic. I think most people shy away from cooking because they see it as this big daunting task. But it's really not. I encourage you to cook in the kitchen and hope I can inspire you to be creative with food. Enjoy what you do, do it with passion and always season with love.
Follow me along, as I create dishes that range from easy and fast to delicate fine dining. But never fear, they are always tasty.
